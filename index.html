How to Learn Guitar

Technique

Your first steps to learning guitar!.



  1. Make sure the guitar is tuned. From left to right, the strings notes are E, A, D, G, B, E.
  2. Hold the guitar on your lap with the neck in your left hand. The guitar should be at an angle with the neck pointed higher than the base. Your right hand will do the strumming, or picking, if you prefer. Make sure you are sitting in an upright position.
  3. If you are using a pick, make a fist with your right hand and hold the pick perpendicular to your fist between your thumb and index finger.
